Super cute giraffes

Giraffes are some of the adorable creatures you can see at the zoo. With many unique features, giraffes are interesting, cute, and special. Their intricate spots and height make them differ from any other animal. At some zoos, you can even feed them! Lettuce is one of their favorite treats. Giraffes are very gentle, so don't be afraid to touch them!

Facts about giraffes

  • Giraffes have long necks to eat the highest morsels of food.
  • Giraffes can run as fast as 35 mph over short distances,but only 10 mph over long ones.
  • Just like human fingerprints, no giraffe's spots are the same. Not a single giraffe has the same pattern
  • Giraffes will fight over females, using their necks as swords!
